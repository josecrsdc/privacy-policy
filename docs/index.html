Mantify

Privacy Policy

Summary

Mantify is an industrial maintenance management application that works entirely locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

Your information never leaves your device.

Data stored by the app

All information is stored locally on your device using SwiftData:

  • Machine information (name, serial number, location, status)
  • Maintenance records (dates, type, priority, costs)
  • Stock inventory (components, quantities, suppliers)
  • Photos captured for documentation
  • App preferences (username, appearance)

Data collection

Mantify does not collect any personal or usage data. Specifically:

  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No analytics services are used
  • No third-party SDKs are integrated
  • No location data is collected
  • No advertising identifiers are used
  • No account creation is required

Device permissions

The app may request access to the camera and photo library only so you can attach images to your records. These photos are stored locally and are not shared with any external service.

Security

Data is stored in the iOS-protected application sandbox. The security of your information depends on your device protection measures (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID).

Data deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the application from your device. Doing so permanently removes all stored information.

Changes to this policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page with the new information.
